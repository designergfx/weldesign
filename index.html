About us

Weldesign L.L.C is a metal fabrication company that offers wide range of fabrication solutions all over the UAE.

We offer a wide range of solutions for commercial, residential, and industrial sectors. Our dedicated team ensures top-quality service, meeting clients' technical, cost, and scheduling needs, regardless of project size or complexity. With skilled field personnel and CAD experts proficient in AutoCAD, STAAD Pro, and Solidworks, we deliver precise and reliable shop drawings for fabrication. We prioritize sustainability and actively contribute to building a sustainable future for our employees, customers, community, and generations to come. Caring deeply for social, economic, and environmental wellbeing, we are committed to utilizing the latest techniques and technology while maintaining practical knowledge gained from years of experience.

Quality is our road to success.

At Weldesign L.L.C quality is an intrinsic part of every activity. Our goal is to provide highest level of construction services at competitive prices, and make sure they are delivered on time, on schedule, and within budget. Our Quality control program is well planned and systematic, and ensure everything is fully in accordance with engineering specifications, codes, and quality plan laid down by specific projects. This is the methodically implemented across four stages – procurement of materials, project execution, and inspection requirements

The safe way is the right way.

As en employer, it is our responsibility to train employees, provide safe workplace and supervise employees to make sure they are working safely. We focus strongly on preventive safety practices, and have formulated strict safety standards. These are applied as broadly as possible to all managers, supervisors, staff, contractors and visitors without exception. But safety has to be everyone’s responsibility if we want ti achieve zero injuries. So all our employers are made responsible for any unsafe behavior, and warned and even disciplined if they are found working unsafely. In this sense, safety is not just instructions, but also about managing company wide change to safe behavior.

Metalwork Applications

Weldesign helps you to deliver these products in different sectors such as medical applications, food processing, energy, construction,architecture, and transportation.

Mezzanine Floor

We've constructed mezzanines and steel decks for schools, warehouses, retail, etc. Our welders are certified and can build mezzanines per your specifications; including welding, grinding, and prime painting, or epoxy paint finishing. We can install your mezzanine or metal deck as allowed by DM. Common options for mezzanines are forklift gates and removable railings.

Steel Stairs & Stairways

We have the equipment and materials to fabricate a variety of star configurations such as spiral staircases, grate stairs, 4', 5' or double wide or pan stairs for concrete pouring. Our certified welders can build stairs per your specifications, including welding; grinding; and prime painting, galvanizing, or epoxy paint finishing.

Railings

The work includes welding, grinding, prime painting, galvanizing, powder coating or epoxy paint finishing. For both indoor and outdoor applications. Metal hand railings can be found in fire exits, public stairs, schools, universities, and balconies with additional interior and exterior applications such as pool fences, guard and stair railings for commercial and residential locations.

Ladders

Our certified welders can build a ladder per your specifications; including welding; grinding; and prime painting, galvanizing, powder coating or epoxy paint finishing. We've constructed ladders for use in schools, warehouses, public buildings and private industry. We have built our reputation on fabricating high quality steel weldments and products for a variety of applications.

Car Sheds

With experience in the fabrication and installation of parking sheds in the UAE. Custom designs according to the customer requirements. The high quality HDPE and PVC shades protects your car from sun and rain. We will help you to select the right parking shed for your car. Weldesign has built its reputation on producing quality structural steel fabrications, such as durable and safe parking sheds.

Platforms & Catwalks

We're reputed on producing quality structural steel fabrications, such as durable and safe catwalk systems, Our highly qualified fabricators are committed to prompt construction of heavy-duty steel walkways. We’ve constructed catwalks for schools, industrial applications,etc. Our state certified welders can build a catwalk according to your specifications, including welding, grinding and epoxy paint finishing.

Stainless Steel Fabrication

Stainless steel is well-known for its heat resistance, biocompatibility, strength, durability, and low maintenance. A minimum of 10.5% chromium content in it provides the formation of a tough, invisible, corrosion-resisting chromium oxide film that adheres to the steel surface Because of its versatility, stainless steel is ideal for a wide range of metal fabrication shop projects
